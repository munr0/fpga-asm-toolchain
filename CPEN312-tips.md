## Digital Logic
**_First half of the course_**

Use [Antares](https://github.com/flandreas/antares) to draw and understand digital circuits!

To greatly simplify the process of writing VHDL for your FPGA board, do yourself a favor and download the corresponding `SystemCD.zip`. With this, you can run `SystemBuilder.exe`, and be fully set with project boilerplate and pin assignments! (no _"New Project Wizard"_ needed)

- https://download.terasic.com/downloads/cd-rom/de0-cv/
- https://download.terasic.com/downloads/cd-rom/de1-soc/
- https://download.terasic.com/downloads/cd-rom/de10-lite/
